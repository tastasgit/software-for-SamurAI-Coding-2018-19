Preliminary Round Tournament
  • There were 45 entries to the contest, among which 42 teams with sucessful compilation participated in the preliminary round.
  • The preliminary round is a round-robin tournament of all 42 teams, i.e., all the teams play against all the other teams.
  • A total of 861 matches (1,722 races) were played in the tournament.
  • The 41 matches of each round are with different opponents and on different race courses.
  • For each match, points given to the teams are:
    • 2 points to the match winner,
    • 1 point each for a draw match, and
    • 0 point to the loser.
    i.e., winning all the 41 matches would result in 82 points.
  • Ranks are decided on the following criteria, in this order.
    • Higher total points
    • Lower total time
  • Teams selected for the final round are:
    • The top-ranked eight teams, and
    • Eight more teams consisting only of members born on or after April 2nd, 1993.
